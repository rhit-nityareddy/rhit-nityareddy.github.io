The Battery Workforce Challenge is a 3 year competition/project that Rose-Hulman participates in. The goal is to build a battery for an electric van. I am part of the Battery Management System Sub-Team, where we test the battery's safety features, notably the High Voltage Interlock Loop (HVIL). We also test the batterus current and temperature to make sure it is at safe levels. We create testbenches using MATLAB Simulink
